# Invenance ERP

**Invenance ERP** is a multi-tenant inventory and accounting management system built to help businesses manage stock and finances in one unified platform.

> Invenance ERP keeps track of your inventory and your finances together in one place, giving you clear insight into your stock and your profits.

---

## What Problem Does It Solve?

Many businesses rely on disconnected tools for inventory and accounting, leading to:

* Inaccurate stock levels
* Manual reconciliation
* Poor visibility into real costs and profitability

Invenance ERP solves this by integrating **real-time inventory operations** with **accounting logic**, enabling accurate tracking, valuation, and reporting.

---

## Key Features

* Real-time inventory tracking (perpetual inventory)
* Product variants and multi-warehouse support
* Integrated accounting (inventory valuation & COGS)
